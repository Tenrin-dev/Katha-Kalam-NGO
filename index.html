    More about

    Making live beautiful, one step at a time"

    Katha Kalam, a Kolkata-based NGO, is dedicated to supporting children with autism and disabilities by creating a safe and inclusive environment. It provides vital resources such as education, therapy, and vocational training to help these children develop essential skills and lead meaningful lives. By collaborating with parents and caregivers, the organization ensures they receive the guidance needed to navigate the unique challenges of raising children with special needs.

    The organization offers programs focused on skill development, including social, emotional, and communication therapy, as well as vocational training in fields like IT, healthcare, and hospitality. Through its efforts, Katha Kalam aims to break societal barriers and foster a more inclusive community where individuals with disabilities can realize their full potential.
